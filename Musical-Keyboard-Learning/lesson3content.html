Scales In The Keyboard .

As a beginner in learning musical keyboard, you might be wondering about ,"What are scales ?"
There's so much to remember when you are first learning how to play the musical keyboard. You need to sure , you learn the basics correctly the first time. so that you will not to go in wrong way to learn musical keyboard. When it comes to learning how to play the musical keyboard, learning things right the first time is much easier than trying to unlearn poor practices and relearn best practices in the future.So you can become a pro in keyboard.

Scales are often a keyboard student's, first step in learning how to play to the musical keyboard. While practicing scales can seem to be a tedious, routine exercise, scales provide the fundamentals for understanding music and mastering practicing techniques.

In general, it's a good idea to start with the major scales first, then move on to the natural minor, harmonic minor, and melodic minor scales. Once you master those you can move onto things like chromatic scales, â€‹blues scales, pentatonic scales and whole tone scales.

What Is A Scale?

You have a doubt while introducing these scale ,"What exactly is a scale and why is it important to learn?"
A scale is a set of musical notes arranged in order which starts from a particular note to the same note repeated.
Scales make up the backbone of most musical pieces, with a single scale providing the melody and harmony.
The scales are in many types.one type of scale acts as a one type of speciality in music.
In types of scales many different scales are there like

  1. Major scales
  2. Natural minor scales
  3. Melodic minor scales
  4. Hormonic minor scales
  5. Blues scales
  6. Pentatonic scales etc.

In above scales,firstly the beginners have to learn mainly two types of scales.

  1. Major scales
  2. Natural minor scales




Because,these two types of scales that major and natural minor scales are the building blocks of learning to play the musical keyboard and also for piano.
There are other types of scales that advanced students may wish to study, including harmonic and melodic.
The remaining scales above we saw can be learn after the beginners level

Major scales
Major scales are basic to your understanding of musical keys and are the scales that most musical students learn first.
This scale consists of 7 notes or tunes

There are 12 major scales
  • C major :
    C D E F G A B C

  • D major :
    D E F# G A B C# D
    D E Gb G A B Db D

  • E major :
    E F# G# A B C# D# E
    E Gb Ab A B Db Eb E

  • F major :
    F G A A# C D E F
    F G A Bb C D E F

  • G major :
    G A B C D E F# G
    G A B C D E Gb G
  • A major :
    A B C# D E F# G# A
    A B Db D E Gb Ab A

  • B major :
    B C# D# E F# G# A# B
    B Db Eb E Gb Ab Bb B

  • C# major :
    C# D# F F# G# A# C C#
    Db Eb F Gb Ab Bb C Db

  • D# major :
    D# F G G# A# C D D#
    Eb F G Ab Bb C D Eb

  • F# major :
    F# G# A# B C# D# F F#
    Gb Ab Bb B Db Eb F Gb

  • G# major :
    G# A# C C# D# F G G#
    Ab Bb C Db Eb F G Ab

  • A# major :
    A# C D D# F G A A#
    Bb C D Eb F G A Bb

what is chromatic scale?

In addition to major and minor scales, there are many other scales in the world of music.
One scale we have to learn that is chromatic scale.It contains all the notes of Western music.
The chromatic scale contains all 12 notes in keyboard that notes such as "C C# D D# E F F# G G# A A# B C".

To find any scale means major,minor scales etc it was used to find scales easily.
To play chromatic scales on a piano keyboard, you must play all the white keys and all the black keys in order, one after another.
